Instant Ramen Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 packet of instant ramen noodles
  • Water
  • Optional: Vegetables (e.g., green onions, carrots, mushrooms)
  • Optional: Protein (e.g., cooked chicken, tofu, eggs)
  • Optional: Seasoning packet included with the ramen

Instructions:

  1. Bring a pot of water to a boil.
  2. Open the packet of instant ramen noodles and place them in the boiling water.
  3. If desired, add any vegetables or protein you'd like to the pot.
  4. Cook for about 2-3 minutes or until the noodles are soft and the vegetables/protein are cooked.
  5. Remove the pot from heat and carefully pour the contents into a bowl.
  6. If desired, add the seasoning packet included with the ramen and stir well.
  7. Let the ramen cool for a minute or two before serving.
  8. Enjoy your delicious instant ramen!
