Instant Ramen Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Take a packet of SHIN RAMEN
  • Water
  • Optional: Vegetables (Green Vegetables so that you add a little Nutritious value.)
  • Optional: Protein (Chicken, Eggs, any kind of meat you prefer.)
  • Seasoning packet (Comes with the Ramen itself.

RECIPE TO A PERFECT RAMEN BOWL

  1. Bring a pot of water to a boil.
  2. Put half of the seasoning in the boiling water.
  3. Add Vegetables and Proteinss.
  4. Cook for 4-8 minutes.
  5. Remove the pot from heat and carefully pour the contents into a bowl.
  6. Add the seasoning packet.
  7. Let the ramen cool for a minute or two before serving.
  8. Enjoy your delicious instant ramen!
